Storm Days in CCRCE

stormday.benjaminmacleod.com

The CCRCE Storm Day Statistics website is an unofficial collection of cancellations, early dismissals, delayed arrivals, and bussing restrictions from the Colchester region of the Chignecto Central Regional Centre for Education (CCRCE).

South Colchester Academy Archives

bit.ly/southcolchesterarchives

SCA Archives is a digital collection of yearbooks, video projects, and additional content. SCA Archives contains records from South Colchester Academy (2003 - Present), as well as the former South Colchester High School (1950 - 2003).
